Country Flairs

When a poster's country differs from the board's home country (derived from its URI prefix), their flair is automatically overridden. Add named rules here — e.g. FR → GB = "Frogs". Boards with no country prefix (e.g. /pol/) are unaffected. Pairs with no rule fall back to the flag emoji + country code.
